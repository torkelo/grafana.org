Grafana provides a powerful and elegant way to create, explore, and share dashboards and data with your team and the world.


Grafana is most commonly used for visualizing time series data for Internet infrastructure and application analytics but many use it in other domains including industrial sensors, home automation, weather, and process control.

Grafana features pluggable panels and data sources allowing easy extensibility and a variety of panels, including fully featured graph panels with rich visualization options. There is currently support for Graphite, InfluxDB, OpenTSDB and KairosDB, and support for SQL is on the roadmap.

Feature Highlights

Rich Graphing

Fully-interactive, editible graphs. Multiple graph types to choose from.

Mixed Styling

Draw your graphs how you want. Mix lines, points and bars. Mix stacked w/ isolated series

Themes

Ships with two themes. If you dodn’t like the default dark theme, switch to a light theme.



Templated Dashboards

Any metric path can be replaced with a template variable.

Templated Queries

We put variables in your variables so you can variable your variables while you variable.

Repeating Panels

Create a dashboards that automatically adds/removes rows and panels



Data Sources

Supports Graphite, InfluxDB, OpenTSDB and KairosDB out of the box. Or use the plug-in functionality to add your own.

Authentication

Manage users, roles and organizations. through LDAP, Basic Auth and Auth Proxy

Annotations

Annotate graphs with rich events from datasouces including Elasticsearch, Graphite and InfluxDB

Snapshot Sharing

Create and share a fully-interactive graphs in 1-click and share with only your team or the world.
